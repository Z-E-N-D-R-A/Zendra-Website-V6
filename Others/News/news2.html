New Era Beginning Soon!!? Zentral-Web v6 Launch, 24-hours Live Stream, and MORE!!!
23 January 2026
Today marks the 3rd anniversary of the channel! Thank you for all your unwavering supports. To celebrate such important milestone, we are excited to announce major plans for the coming year that will change the course of Zendra forever!
The New Era of Zendra
We are proud to present Era of Zentrals, beginning on February 23rd, 2026, marking the sixth era of Zendra. This era begins with Chapter I - Stellar Ignition, that will bring you to the world of Zentrals and Kura. Chapter II and III will follow and released in Summer and Winter 2026 respectively.
This new era comes with two Mega Projects, Project Zentral-Web and Project Starr.
Project Zentral-Web is the sixth version of Zendra Website and will replace v5. More details about it below.
Project Starr is a fantasy project with two parts. Part one will drop with Chapter II of Era of Zentrals, and part two will drop with Chapter III. More information will be published in Project Page in the future.
Launch of Zentral-Web v6.0
After months of development, we are excited to announce the release of Zentral-Web v6.0 (this). This website introduces many new features, with Zentral-Chat being the biggest of all. Zentral-Chat is a free-to-use online chat service, allowing Zentrals from all around the world to connect and chat. Try it now in Zentral-Chat! No login is required and you can chat whenever and wherever you want.
We plan on continuing development and improving the website. Zentral-Web v6.0.1 will drop in a month with bug fixes. Version 6.1 will also come in June 2026. If you find any bugs or have any suggestions, please report to Feedback Form.
24-hours Live Stream
To celebrate the Z-Week and the channel's 3rd anniversary, I will be live streaming on YouTube for 24 hours on January 29th at 8pm EST! This will be the longest live stream ever on the channel and will be a big part of Zendra. Join the live stream to play with me!
